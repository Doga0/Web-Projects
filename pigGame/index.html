How To Play?

Each turn, a player click the ROLL DICE button until either a 1 is rolled or player decides to hold.

If the player rolls a 1, their current score will be 0 and the total score will not increase. It becomes the next player's turn.

If the player rolls any other number, it is added to their score and the player's turn continues.

If the player chooses to hold, their current score is added to their score and it becomes the next player's turn.
